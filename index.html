FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Oceanix Hospitality?

Oceanix Hospitality is a specialized skill development and career guidance centre empowering aspiring professionals for the global cruise and hospitality industry. We provide comprehensive training, interview preparation, and placement guidance for luxury cruise lines including Royal Caribbean, MSC Cruises, Celebrity Cruises, and premium hotel brands like Marriott International, The Ritz-Carlton, and Hilton Hotels.

Where are Oceanix Hospitality cruise ship training centers located?

Oceanix Hospitality has training centers conveniently located at MG Road, Kochi, Kerala and Belapur, Mumbai, Maharashtra. Our cruise ship training centres in Kerala and Mumbai serve candidates from all across India looking for international hospitality careers.

Which cruise lines and hotels does Oceanix place students in?

Our students have been successfully placed in top cruise lines including Royal Caribbean International, MSC Cruises, Celebrity Cruises, and prestigious hotel brands like Marriott International, The Ritz-Carlton, and Hilton Hotels worldwide. We specialize in cruise ship jobs, 5-star hotel placements, and international hospitality careers.

What departments does Oceanix Hospitality offer cruise ship training for?

We offer comprehensive cruise ship career training across multiple departments: Security, Food Production (Executive Chef, Sous Chef, Pastry Chef), Food & Beverage Service (Waiter, Bartender, Sommelier), Housekeeping (Cabin Steward, Laundry), Accounts/Finance, Front Office (Receptionist, Concierge), Kitchen Management, Photography, Medical (Doctor, Nurse), Spa & Salon (Massage Therapist, Hair Stylist), and Sales (Retail, Duty Free).

How do I apply for cruise ship jobs through Oceanix Hospitality?

Applying is simple! Click the "Apply Now" button on our website, download our admission form, or contact us directly via WhatsApp at +91 94464 81424 or email at Offical@Oceanixhospitality.info. Our admission process includes: 1) Free Consultation, 2) Professional Training & Grooming, 3) Interview Preparation with mock sessions, and 4) Placement Support with dedicated application guidance.

Do I need a CDC (Continuous Discharge Certificate) for cruise ship jobs?

While a CDC is required for certain maritime positions, Oceanix Hospitality guides you through the entire process including CDC application, STCW certification, and all necessary documentation. Whether you're a fresher or experienced professional, we provide end-to-end support for your merchant navy and cruise ship career journey.
